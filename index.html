Game Details

Explosive chain multipliers with instant volatility.

Chain Reactor is a fast-paced crash-style instant-win game built around timing, tension, and escalating risk.

Gameplay

Players press the Charge button to start the round. Energy chains begin to build vertically, increasing the active multiplier in real time. The longer the chain continues, the higher the potential win.

At any moment, the player must decide when to stop the chain. If the player cashes out before the system crashes, the current multiplier is locked in as a win. If the chain crashes before the player stops it, the round ends immediately with no payout.

The challenge lies in judging how far the chain will rise before collapse, creating a constant risk-versus-reward decision loop.

Why It Works

  • Familiar crash-style mechanic
  • Real-time multiplier escalation
  • Player-controlled cash-out
  • High tension per second
  • Instant round resolution
  • Unique autoplay functionality unavailable in standard crash games

Autoplay Modes (Concept Gaming Exclusive)

Chain Reactor includes a proprietary autoplay system designed to support different player risk profiles:

  • Standard Autoplay – Automatically triggers rounds using default cash-out behaviour for fast, hands-free play.
  • Safe Mode Autoplay – Focuses on lower-risk play by cashing out earlier in the chain, delivering smoother session flow and reduced volatility.
  • High-Volatility Autoplay – Pushes deeper into chain progression before cash-out, maximising risk exposure and potential reward.

These autoplay modes are exclusive to Concept Gaming and allow players to tailor volatility without changing core gameplay mechanics.
